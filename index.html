What's the Deal with _____?

What's the Deal with ______? is a weekly podcast summarizing important issues in current events.

Instead of focusing on the latest headlines and the 24 hour cycle, we create summaries, providing background on major actors, timelines, and historical context in 15-30 minutes.

What's the Deal with Brexit?

July 11 2016

Brexit is a portmanteau of British Exit and refers to the recent British referendum to leave the EU.

Find out what happened, who did what, and where things stand now.

What's the Deal with #blacklivesmastter?

July 04 2016

#blacklivesmatter is a social media campaign to draw attention to the disproportionate incidents of violence and death between white police officers and black citizens in the United States.

Find out what happened, who did what, and where things stand now.
